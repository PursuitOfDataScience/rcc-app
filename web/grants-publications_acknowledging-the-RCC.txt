Title: Acknowledging the RCC | Research Computing Center
================================================================================
If you have published a paper or have given a presentation that acknowledges the Research Computing Center 's (RCC) computational resources or other services, please email us with the paper title, journal, or conference where a presentation was given. These citations help the RCC demonstrate the vital role of computational resources and support staff in research at the University of Chicago.
If you would like your RCC-supported work featured on our website, please indicate that you are comfortable with us doing so.
In citations and acknowledgments, please reference the RCC as "the University of Chicago's Research Computing Center." Some example citations are shown below.
This work was completed in part with resources provided by the University of Chicago's Research Computing Center.
We are grateful for the support of the University of Chicago's Research Computing Center for assistance with the calculations carried out in this work.
We acknowledge the University of Chicago's Research Computing Center for their support of this work.
