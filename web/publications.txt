Beagle3 and the RCC's resources and staff support a number of faculty projects that have resulted in published academic papers.
If you have published a paper that acknowledged RCC please contact us with the appropriate citation information.
For information on how to cite or acknowledge RCC in your publications see:
If you have published a paper or have given a presentation that acknowledges the RCC's computational resources or other services, please e-mail us with the paper title, journal, or conference where a presentation was given. These citations help the RCC demonstrate the role of computational resources and support staff in research at UChicago. If you would like your RCC-supported work featured on our website, please indicate that you are comfortable with us doing so.
In citations and acknowledgements, please reference the RCC as "the University of Chicago's Research Computing Center." Some example citations are shown below.
This work was completed in part with resources provided by the University of Chicago's Research Computing Center.
We are grateful for the support of the University of Chicago's Research Computing Center for assistance with the calculations carried out in this work.
We acknowledge the University of Chicago's Research Computing Center for their support of this work.
