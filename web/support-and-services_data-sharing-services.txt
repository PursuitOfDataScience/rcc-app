Title: Data Sharing Services | Research Computing Center
================================================================================
The Research Computing Center (RCC) provides a centralized infrastructure where all resources are accessible by multiple users simultaneously. This makes RCC's storage system ideal for sharing data among research group members.
Additionally, data access and restriction levels can be put in place on an extremely granular level.
Learn more about RCC's storage and backup and systems and practices.
