Title: New Faculty Program | Research Computing Center
================================================================================
The Research Computing Center (RCC) provides free access to research computing infrastructure to all faculty members of the University of Chicago. If you are an incoming or prospective faculty member of the University of Chicago, the RCC can help make your transition as seamless as possible by providing access to our high-performance computing and data storage resources in advance of your arrival on campus.
