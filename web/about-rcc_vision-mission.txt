Title: Vision & Mission | Research Computing Center
================================================================================
Vision
To transform and enable research, education, and discovery at UChicago.
Mission
To advance research and scholarship by providing access to high-end computing and data resources, training, and advanced technical support.
Key mission statements:
* Research - To enable and transform UChicago's research and scholarship enterprise through access to computing, data resources, and expertise.
* Education - To equip faculty and students with vital computational research skills through customizable training and educational opportunities.
* Discovery - To cultivate cutting-edge technologies and institutional collaborations that enable discovery and increase the competitiveness of our research and talent.
