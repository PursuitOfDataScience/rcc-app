Title: Research Computing Oversight Committee | Research Computing Center
================================================================================
The Research Computing Oversight Committee was created to oversee the development and execution of a comprehensive University-wide research computing strategy, along with attendant policies, and to provide advice to the vice provost for research and the director of research computing on research computing challenges and opportunities.
